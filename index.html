Our process, in a nutshell

At a high level, these are the steps an “idea” takes from initial conception to final delivery.

Idea
submitted
Could come from anywhere – a customer, the market, a competitive analysis, or a back-of-the-napkin epiphany.
→
Idea
reviewed
We gather weekly to review all the new ideas, ask clarifying questions, etc.
→
Idea
scored
Ideas are scored against business value, effort, and strategic fit.
→
Roadmap
planned
Takes all existing “ideas” and fits them into upcoming sprints.
→
Idea
developed
This includes QA testing and stakeholder reviews.
→
Idea
rolled out
Release notes, training, and customer comms.
