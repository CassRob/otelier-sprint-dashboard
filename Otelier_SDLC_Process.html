Ideation Process
Product Intake
🔄 Cross-functional review  2× / month
Input Sources
👥 Customer & internal stakeholder requests
📊 Market research & competitive analysis
🎯 Strategic business goals
💡 New revenue-generating product capabilities
Intake Outcomes
Short-term roadmap — High priority; planned delivery ≤ 6 months
Long-term roadmap — Considered in next quarterly planning; no timeline guaranteed
Professional Services — Customer funds custom delivery via SOW
Rejected — Not in scope at this time
Planning Process
Roadmap Prioritization
📅 Quarterly commitment & planning
Quarterly Cadence
1
Month 1 — Review & Carry-Over
Review items delivered in the previous quarter and complete any carry-over work
2
Month 2 — Prioritize & Plan
Prioritize the backlog and plan the next quarter plus the next half of the year
3
Month 3 — Publish Commitment
Publish next quarter as committed roadmap; next half as tentative plan
📐 Prioritization Framework
Strategic fit · Customer value · Applicability across use-cases · Platform synergy · ROI / Business impact · Demand vs. Capacity
Exception Process
Adaptive Strategy Council
⚡ Fortnightly exception review
Formally reviews and decides on exceptions to established product strategy or engineering priorities. Minimum threshold: $50K ARR impact.
Escalation Use Cases
⚡ Accelerate an accepted roadmap item

Delivery & Release Cadences

Three time horizons from sprint execution to customer communication

⚙️ Sprint Cadence
Every 2 Weeks
▸Engineering team executes planned backlog items within 2-week sprint cycles
▸Sprint planning, daily standups, sprint review & retrospective
▸Code complete, tested, and merged into release candidate branch
▸Continuous integration & automated testing throughout
✅ Tested Code Ready for Release
📋 Monthly Release
Every Month
▸Accumulated sprint work deployed to production
▸Product Owner compiles internal release notes summarizing all work completed and released in the past month
▸Distributed internally to keep all teams aligned on product progress and changes
📋 Internal Release Notes
by Product Owner
🎯 Quarterly Release
Every Quarter
▸Roadmap milestone deliverables committed at quarter start are released to customers
▸External release notes published, mapped against declared roadmap milestones
▸Demo videos produced for all customer-facing features released in the quarter
